Business & Social Media
Specialist

RE IMAGE is looking for motivated, self-driven individuals to join our growing platform as Media & Business Specialists. In this role, you will create engaging content for local businesses while also helping them discover opportunities to grow through RE IMAGE services. This is a flexible, part-time opportunity designed for individuals who want to earn based on performance, build relationships, and grow their income over time.

Job Overview

We are building a modern, performance-driven platform for local business growth. This role combines content creation, business relationship building, and long-term earning potential in one lane.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Visit local businesses and create social media content
  • Produce 3–5 pieces of content per session, including at least 3 videos
  • Complete content sessions within 1–2 hours
  • Collaborate with businesses on social media posts such as Instagram and Facebook
  • Upload content in original quality using Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Identify areas where businesses can improve, including content, website, branding, and growth opportunities
  • Introduce businesses to RE IMAGE services without hard selling

Lead & Growth Structure

  • Leads are not guaranteed at the start
  • Opportunities are earned through performance
  • You are encouraged to prospect your own clients
  • Creators who perform well receive more opportunities over time

What We’re Looking For

  • Minimum 1,000 followers on at least one social media platform
  • Ability to create engaging content using a phone or camera
  • Strong communication and people skills
  • Self-motivated and comfortable approaching businesses
  • Willing to learn and grow within a performance-based system

Perks & Benefits

  • Flexible schedule with true part-time freedom
  • Discounts through RE IMAGE partner businesses
  • Performance-based rewards and recognition
  • Earn $60,000+ personally and qualify for a company trip

Important Notes

  • This is a 1099 independent contractor role
  • Income is not hourly or guaranteed
  • Earnings are based on activity, performance, and consistency

Compensation

Content Sessions$100 per session lasting 1–2 hours. Sessions depend on business demand and are not guaranteed daily.
Performance Earnings From ContentEarn about 5% of sales generated from your content using a unique tracking code.
Website SalesEarn 25% upfront commission on every website sold.
Website Monthly IncomeEarn 5% recurring monthly income from website management.
Additional ServicesEarn 15% upfront on other services such as branding and AI, plus 5% recurring on ongoing services.
FundingEarn from helping businesses get approved for funding through RE IMAGE connections.

Earning Potential (Part-Time)

  • Year 1: about $60,000
  • Year 2: about $90,000
  • Year 3: about $120,000
  • Year 4: about $160,000
  • Year 5: up to $200,000

Growth is driven by recurring income, repeat clients, and increased opportunities over time.

Why Join RE IMAGE?

  • Multiple income streams, not just one paycheck
  • Opportunity to build long-term recurring income
  • Real growth potential without income caps
  • Be part of a modern, scalable business model
