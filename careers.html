Sales Representative Opportunity

RE IMAGE is looking for motivated sales representatives to help local businesses modernize, digitize, and eliminate redundant work. This flexible remote/local sales role pays 20% commission on approved closed deals. Applicants must complete onboarding and pass the salesman test before dashboard access is unlocked.

What You’ll Sell

Sell practical business systems: websites, admin portals, client portals, automations, booking workflows, invoice systems, QR code systems, full digital transformation packages.

What You’ll Be Doing

  • Prospect local and remote businesses that need cleaner digital systems
  • Explain RE IMAGE services clearly and professionally
  • Sell websites, portals, automations, booking systems, invoice systems, QR tools, and business workflows
  • Submit leads, notes, invoice requests, and QR requests through the salesman portal
  • Follow up consistently and keep admin updated on deal status
  • Protect the brand by avoiding promises that require admin approval

Training & Dashboard Access

  • Create a salesman portal account after applying
  • Track your application status from the portal
  • Admin must approve your application before onboarding unlocks
  • Complete the onboarding guide and pass the test with at least 85%
  • Your dashboard unlocks only after approval, onboarding completion, and passing score

What We’re Looking For

  • Strong communication and follow-up discipline
  • Comfortable approaching owners and decision makers
  • Interest in websites, business systems, automation, and digital transformation
  • Ability to document client needs clearly
  • Willingness to complete onboarding before selling independently

Perks & Benefits

  • 20% commission on approved closed deals
  • Flexible hours with remote/local selling options
  • Sell practical, high-value systems businesses actually need
  • Admin can assign leads as performance grows
  • Track commissions, requests, notes, and follow-ups in your portal

Important Notes

  • This is a 1099 independent contractor role
  • Income is not hourly or guaranteed
  • Earnings are based on activity, performance, and consistency
  • Dashboard access is gated until application approval, onboarding completion, and passing exam score
